Learn

The Five Elements are living archetypes of energy in both nature and self. Each one holds wisdom, gifts, and imbalances. At Lumen, we translate these ancient concepts into everyday tools that meet you where you are.

  • Wood: Vision, ambition, growth
  • Fire: Passion, inspiration, heart-led expression
  • Earth: Grounding, nourishment, harmony
  • Metal: Clarity, structure, letting go
  • Water: Intuition, flow, inner knowing

Each element corresponds to tools, crystals, and practices that bring that energy to life. We’re here to guide your journey of reconnection, one ritual at a time.
