Five Element Energies

Understanding the Five Elements helps you restore harmony with natural forces. Each element corresponds to specific crystals and inner traits.

  • Wood – Growth, Creativity – Best with Green Aventurine, Amazonite
  • Fire – Passion, Expression – Best with Carnelian, Garnet
  • Earth – Stability, Nurturing – Best with Citrine, Tiger’s Eye
  • Metal – Precision, Wisdom – Best with Clear Quartz, Hematite
  • Water – Intuition, Depth – Best with Amethyst, Lapis Lazuli
